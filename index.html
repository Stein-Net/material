About-Me

I began taking things apart as a child, starting with flashlights. I would take them apart and see 'what made them tick'. Eventually I moved on to bigger things, until one day my dad and I took a computer apart.

As I grew up, my mother had the philosophy that if she couldn't answer my question, we could do a little research and find the answer out. This gave me a curious edge- and thanks to my already existing background in computers I began researching the computers that I was doing research on!

I began Android Development when I was in middle school, and in High School I had my first 'professional' gig. I designed the JROTC App for my school (Christian Brothers Academy). It was a simple enough Android app, displaying the ranks of the senior officers etc. And I maintained it through my Senior year, and have passed it down to a junior at the school. It was also in my Senior year that I recieved my first Mac from my Uncle Gary- and thus began my iOS and Mac development.

Now! I am a student at SUNY Polytechnic Institute in New York. :)
